Why We Are

SOS Hermann Gmeiner Schools are established in conjunction with SOS Children’s Villages and provide quality education to children from the villages as well as underprivileged children from the local community. The schools believe that a good education is the greatest gift that can be given to children. Character building and personality development are of utmost importance in these schools, so 21st-century skills are imparted to both faculty and students. The schools follow the National Education Policy and are equipped with STEM Labs, Computer Labs, and other required infrastructure, including sprawling sports facilities
